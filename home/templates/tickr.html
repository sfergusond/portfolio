Tickr

My client's goal for Tickr was a daily/weekly fantasy stock simulation game - akin to fantasy games such as Fantasy Football. The client desired a clean and simple web-based application for users around the world to pit their chosen stocks against each other in league-style and head-to-head match ups.

The result us a fast, reliable, smoothly functioning site with modern visuals and a readable layout. As the developer, I struck a balance between performance and frugality. Upon completion, I delivered an secure, easily-scalable, and SEO optimized site with backend costs of only $14 per month.
