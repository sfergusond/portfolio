A sleek a minimalist gallery/eCommerce site for a Hawaii-based artist. Contains the following:

  • Home (splash) screen
  • About page
  • Contact page with email integration
  • Gallery/product browsing page, with a search/filter/sort function and detail/grid viewing modes
  • Item detail pages with photo carousels, product descriptions, and a "Buy" button
  • Payment screen with Stripe integration. Takes shipping/billing addresses, customer name/email, and payment information. Automatically captures payments (or redirects to an error page if unsuccessful) and send receipts to the site owner and customer
  • Admin portal to view, edit, and create products
