Slice.fm

View

Slice.fm began as a web application for podcasters. The initial incaration of the website allowed podcasters to register, choose a subscription status, and use the trailer-maker tool to easily make, customize, and share trailers for their podcasts. Using a range of AWS services, and Google Cloud Speech-to-Text, I built a scalable application capable of producing hundred of podcast trailers simulaneously. See the features page for more information.

Slice.fm is currently being revamped. The current goal for the site is to become a marketplace usable by both podcasters and businesses interested in leveraging podcasts as an advertisement platform. The marketplace will allow podcasters and advertisers to search and connect with one another, for advertisers to begin marketing campaigns, and to securely handle cross-currency payments. Find out more about Slice.fm's goals and potential earnings here.
